Add a sequence of initial guess points between two waypoints. The points are inserted between the waypoints at fromIndex and fromIndex + 1. Linear interpolation between the waypoint initial guess points and these segment initial guess points is used as the initial guess of the robot's pose over the trajectory.

Parameters
from_indexindex of the waypoint the initial guess point comes immediately after
sgmt_pose_guessthe sequence of initial guess points
